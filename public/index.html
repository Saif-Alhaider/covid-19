COVID-19 Ventilator Status App using Blockchain


When the whole world is battling against the #COVID19 pandemic, leaders are thinking how to bring economiesback into track and healthcare workers playing on their lives to help save human lives; It is our duty as Citizen developers to engage in crafting great ideas into real-life soultions so as to ease every challenge posed to us.

This is a blockchain powered app to provide an interactive and easy map to view ventilator counts and statuses worldwide through a simple dashboard. We invite hospitals to login and update ventilator counts in realtime. Click on See Coverage to view the map. For more information about the project, visit the github repository
